Why I want to become a developer?

Many people are drawn to becoming software developers because it combines creativity with problem-solving in a highly dynamic field. The ability to build innovative solutions, from apps to websites, offers both a sense of accomplishment and creative expression. Additionally, the tech industry provides strong career opportunities, job security, and the potential for high salaries, making it an attractive option for many. The constant evolution of technology also appeals to those who enjoy continuous learning and growth. For others, the flexibility of remote work and the chance to make a real impact through their creations are key motivators.
